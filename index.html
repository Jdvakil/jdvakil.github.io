About me

I am a Robotic System Integrator at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennslyvania. I am mentored by Vikash Kumar and closely collaborate with Homanga Bharadhwaj. I build large-scale multi-task agents that learn from structured visual representations.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science and Math from the University of Washington.

I am interested in areas of Robotics, Embodied Artificial Intelligence (EAI), and solving autonomous world navigation. Besides robotics and research, I am interested in climbing/bouldering, photography, football(soccer), and cars.
